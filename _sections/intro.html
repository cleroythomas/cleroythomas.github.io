ATX Maps

This site focuses on Civic App soulutions, History Story Maps, and Data Visualizations. It will offer 4 major solutions a year and several story maps a month. The Spring/2018 solution is a Homelessness Reduction Suite of apps (see below). The Summer/2018 Apps will focus on Citizen Flood Safety. Current story maps include a Oakwood Cemetery App , a Famous Texas Trees Tour, and a Tour of the East Austin Tejano Trails
